MASTERY TEST: NURSING CARE - MORNING ROUTINE

KEY CHALLENGE:
Effectively monitor and report on a resident's physical state.

VOCABULARY TO TEST:
1. 顔色 (Kaoiro) - Complexion
2. 食欲 (Shokuyoku) - Appetite
3. 薬 (Kusuri) - Medicine
4. 報告 (Hōkoku) - Report

CONTEXT LOGIC:
- Check the resident's "顔色" (Kaoiro) as soon as they wake up.
- If they have no "食欲" (Shokuyoku), you must "報告" (Hōkoku) to the head nurse.
- Ensure the "薬" (Kusuri) is taken exactly at 8:00 AM.
