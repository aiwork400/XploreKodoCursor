STUDY MATERIAL: N5 CARE-GIVING - BASIC NURSING & GREETINGS

KEY VOCABULARY (重要語彙):
1. 体調 (Taichō) - Physical condition/Health state.
2. お大事に (O-daiji ni) - Please take care (get well soon).
3. 車椅子 (Kuruma-isu) - Wheelchair.
4. 入浴 (Nyūyoku) - Bathing.
5. 介助 (Kaijo) - Assistance/Care-giving support.
6. 水分補給 (Suibun hokyū) - Hydration/Drinking water.

CORE CONCEPTS (核心概念):
1. COMMUNICATION: Always check the resident's "体調" (Taichō) every morning.
2. HYDRATION: Ensure elderly residents maintain "水分補給" (Suibun hokyū) during hot days.
3. SAFETY: When using a "車椅子" (Kuruma-isu), always lock the brakes before "介助" (Kaijo).
4. EMPATHY: After a session, always say "お大事に" (O-daiji ni) to show respect.
