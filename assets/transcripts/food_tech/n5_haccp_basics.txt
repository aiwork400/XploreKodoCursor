STUDY MATERIAL: N5 FOOD/TECH - HACCP BASICS

KEY VOCABULARY (重要語彙):
1. 食中毒 (Shokuchūdoku) - Food poisoning.
2. 手洗い (Tearai) - Hand washing.
3. 温度管理 (Ondo kanri) - Temperature control.
4. 交差汚染 (Kōsa osen) - Cross-contamination.
5. 消毒 (Shōdoku) - Disinfection.
6. 冷蔵庫 (Reizōko) - Refrigerator.

CORE CONCEPTS (核心概念):
1. HYGIENE: Thorough "手洗い" (Tearai) is the first step to prevent "食中毒" (Shokuchūdoku).
2. STORAGE: "温度管理" (Ondo kanri) is critical; keep raw meat in the "冷蔵庫" (Reizōko) below 10°C.
3. CROSS-CONTAMINATION: Avoid "交差汚染" (Kōsa osen) by using different cutting boards for meat and vegetables.
