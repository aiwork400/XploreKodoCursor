MASTERY TEST: HACCP COMPLIANCE - KITCHEN SAFETY

KEY CHALLENGE:
Apply hygiene protocols during a busy shift to prevent contamination.

VOCABULARY TO TEST:
1. 清潔 (Seiketsu) - Cleanliness/Hygiene
2. 汚染 (Osen) - Contamination
3. 手袋 (Tebukuro) - Gloves
4. 確認 (Kakunin) - Confirmation/Verification

CONTEXT LOGIC:
- Every morning, "清潔" (Seiketsu) check is mandatory.
- If you touch raw meat, you must change your "手袋" (Tebukuro) to avoid "汚染" (Osen).
- Always "確認" (Kakunin) the fridge temperature every 4 hours.
