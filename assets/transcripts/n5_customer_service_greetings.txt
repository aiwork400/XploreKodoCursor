STUDY MATERIAL: N5 CUSTOMER SERVICE GREETINGS

Key Vocabulary (Goi):

いらっしゃいませ (Irasshaimase) - Welcome (to a shop/restaurant)

かしこまりました (Kashikomarimashita) - Certainly / Understood

少々お待ちください (Shōshō omachi kudasai) - Please wait a moment

ありがとうございました (Arigatō gozaimashita) - Thank you very much (past)

Core Concepts:

Use いらっしゃいませ (Irasshaimase) immediately when a customer enters the Commercial Center.

When a customer makes a request, respond with かしこまりました (Kashikomarimashita) to show professional understanding.

If you need to check stock or a price, say 少々お待ちください (Shōshō omachi kudasai).
