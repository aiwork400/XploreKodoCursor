STUDY MATERIAL: N5 CUSTOMER SERVICE & CASHIER OPERATIONS

KEY VOCABULARY (重要語彙):
1. いらっしゃいませ (Irasshaimase) - Welcome (standard greeting for customers).
2. お会計 (O-kaikei) - The bill/payment process.
3. 現金 (Genkin) - Cash.
4. クレジットカード (Kurejitto kādo) - Credit card.
5. レシート (Reshīto) - Receipt.
6. 少々お待ちください (Shōshō omachi kudasai) - Please wait a moment.
7. お待たせいたしました (Omatase itashimashita) - Thank you for waiting.

CORE CONCEPTS (核心概念):
1. GREETING: Always use "いらっしゃいませ" with a polite bow when a customer enters the shop.
2. PAYMENT: When starting the "お会計" (payment), ask the customer if they prefer "現金" (cash) or "クレジットカード" (credit card).
3. SERVICE: If you need to step away to check a price or get a bag, always say "少々お待ちください".
4. COMPLETION: After handing over the "レシート" (receipt), bow and say "ありがとうございました" (Thank you very much).

SCENARIO EXAMPLE:
Staff: いらっしゃいませ！ (Welcome!)
Customer: This item, please.
Staff: かしこまりました。お会計は1,500円です。現金ですか？ (Understood. The total is 1,500 yen. Will it be cash?)
Customer: Credit card, please.
Staff: 少々お待ちください... お待たせいたしました。こちらレシートです。 (Please wait a moment... Thank you for waiting. Here is your receipt.)
