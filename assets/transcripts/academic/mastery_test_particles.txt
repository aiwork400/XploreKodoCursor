MASTERY TEST: JLPT N5 - WA VS. GA (は vs が)

KEY CHALLENGE:
Distinguish between the topic marker (は) and the subject marker (が) in a classroom setting.

VOCABULARY TO TEST:
1. 教室 (Kyōshitsu) - Classroom
2. 先生 (Sensei) - Teacher
3. 静か (Shizuka) - Quiet
4. 質問 (Shitsumon) - Question

CONTEXT LOGIC:
- Use "は" for known information: 先生は教室にいます。 (The teacher is in the classroom.)
- Use "が" for new information/emphasis: 誰が先生ですか？ (Who is the teacher?)
