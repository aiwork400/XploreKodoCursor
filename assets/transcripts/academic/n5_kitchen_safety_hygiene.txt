STUDY MATERIAL: N5 KITCHEN SAFETY AND HYGIENE

Key Vocabulary (Goi):

衛生 (Eisei) - Hygiene/Sanitation

安全靴 (Anzen-gutsu) - Safety Shoes

清掃 (Seisō) - Cleaning

手洗い (Tearai) - Hand washing

温度管理 (Ondo Kanri) - Temperature Control

Core Concepts:

Always wear 安全靴 (Anzen-gutsu) to prevent slips and foot injuries.

衛生 (Eisei) is maintained through frequent 手洗い (Tearai), especially after touching raw meat.

清掃 (Seisō) must be performed daily on all surfaces.

Food safety requires strict 温度管理 (Ondo Kanri) for refrigerators (below 5°C).
